The Best Movies According to Akhil

My top 3 Movies of all-time

The avangers -Endgame

Avengers: Endgame is a thrilling culmination of the Marvel Cinematic Universe,
where the Avengers unite for a final battle against Thanos.
It’s an emotional and action-packed journey that delivers closure, sacrifice, and heroism.

KGF-2

KGF Chapter 2 is a high-octane action film that follows Rocky as he rises to power and battles
formidable enemies to protect his empire. With intense drama and stunning visuals, it elevates
the saga to a grander scale.

Jersey

Jersey is an emotional sports drama about a struggling ex-cricketer who returns to the
game for his son’s happiness. It’s a heartfelt tale of redemption, love, and chasing dreams
against all odds.
